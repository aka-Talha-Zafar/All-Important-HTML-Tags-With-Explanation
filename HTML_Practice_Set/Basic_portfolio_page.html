About Me:

Meet "Talha" a computer science student powered by caffeine, curiosity, and a borderline unhealthy obsession with clean code and mobile apps. He's the kind of person who dual-boots Windows and Ubuntu just because he can, and then wonders why Ubuntu doesn't love his touchscreen back. With dreams taller than his GitHub contribution graph and a Surface Laptop that’s seen more experiments than a chemistry lab.

Talha is grinding through the world of React Native, slowly cloning Facebook one pixel at a time. He's got startup ideas, AI chatbot prototypes, and a growing collection of repositories titled things like “Beginner’s Guide to Git” and “Why CSS Is My Nemesis.” Whether he's breaking down cloud architecture or debugging why router.back() is ghosting him, Talha does it all with professional ambition, a touch of sarcasm, and a head full of questions like “What if this app could actually diagnose your cold?” Don’t be fooled—behind that humble syntax lies a developer with real hustle, two free years to conquer the dev world, and just enough skepticism to keep tech trends in check.
